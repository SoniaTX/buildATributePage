Tribute to Steve Jobs

The man who brought technological innovations into world and transformed the way the world uses technology

Here's a timeline of Steve Jobs' life:

1955

Born in San Francisco and got adopted by by Paul and Clara Jobs.

1960

Moved to Mountain View (Silicon Valley area) with his family.

1972

Attended Reed College then dropped out after a semester.

1974

Traveled through India to seek enlightenment

1976

Co-founded Apple with Steve Wozniak to sell Apple 1 personal computer.

1983

Launched Apple Lisa after his first child Lisa and continued to expand Apple line.

1984

Introduced Macintosh at Apple Shareholders meeting.

1985

Stripped of CEO title and went on to found NeXT.

1986

Bought computer animation division of George Lucas' company and incorproated it as Pixar.

1991

Married Laurene Powell and later went on to have three children.

1996

Apple purchased Pixar.

1997

Took over at Apple as interim CEO.

1998

Introduced iMac then it became best-selling computer.

2001

Launched iPod.

2003

Diagnosed with pancreatic cancer.

2007

Unveiled the first iPhone.

2010

Apple launched iPad.

2011

Died at the age of 56.
